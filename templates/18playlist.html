18playlist

Katie, I created this virtual mixtape for our 8th wedding anniversary (and 18 years together -- now more than half our lives!). For each one of these songs, I can clearly picture the moments all those years ago when we listened to these together. They are roughly in order from high school years, to Clemson/GT years, to working life and our first home, and finally where we are now with our two beautiful daughters. I'm sure there are so many more songs, but I kept it to 18. Which ones did I miss? I love you!

1) Blink 182 - Josie

I think this is a good place to start. Listening to this in my white Jeep Grand Cherokee. The live album is probably more accurate, but I think this one in particular makes more sense as the kickoff to this mix.

2) Unwritten Law - Up All Night

This one reminds me of you, before we started really dating. This was a CD that you had in your Eclipse. You also had a blue Unwritten Law t-shirt?

3) Fenix TX - A Song for Everyone

If only I could have this song start as soon as I close the car door heading off for a drive. This one reminds me of driving around with you at night, probably with absolutely nothing to else to do.

4) Yellowcard - Drifting

For some reason, I remember playing the first few seconds of this song over and over again before actually letting the song play completely. I'm not sure why I did that, but also hey -- why not?

5) Tom Petty - Mary Jane's Last Dance

We listened to this one just a few times. Eventually we also had the Greatest Hits CD and saw Sir Tom Petty himself (and the Heartbreakers) in concert together.

6) The Movielife - Kelly Song

This one made the cut and I couldn't say why for sure. We didn't looove Movielife exactly -- but I do believe I burned you this album in my room in Archdale and clearly remember making some sweet CD art with a sharpie. That CD may be in our garage somewhere... (update: i wasn't able to find it but I did find these gems.)

7) The Used - A Box Full of Sharp Objects

I think The Used serves as a solid transition from high school to college years. We saw them on the big stage at Warped Tour and the singer climbed to the top of the speaker cabinets -- way up there.

8) Minus The Bear - The Game Needed Me

Lots of Minus The Bear in the Eclipse and eventually Passat. I bought this CD at a Best Buy, probably right after we sat in Barnes and Noble -- studying for classes.

9) Killswitch Engage - The End of Hearthache

We saw Killswitch, but I can't seem to remember if they were the headline. Definitely remember them playing this song. I want to say it was in Charlotte -- Tremont?

10) Appleseed Cast - Fishing in the Sky

I'm not sure if you will associate as many memories of this one -- but I definitely do. I think this was one track on some random mix CD that was stuck in your car for a long time. I would play this one pretty much every time you drove me around somewhere :)

11) Circa Survive - In Fear and Faith

I want to say that we listened to this a bunch while we lived in our first apartment at College Street. Or at least, that's always what I picture when I hear anything from this album.

12) Rise Against - Swing Life Away

I remember listening to this song in college with you and I would wonder what life would look like in another 5/10/20 years. I was hopeful that we'd be able to slow down and enjoy life together -- swinging on a porch looking out at the water...

13) Frou Frou - Let Go

This was classic light music we'd put on in Tenside while either you or I (or both) were studying. I probably played it from my phone that first night we were in Tenside -- when we slept on the floor because we didn't have any furniture yet.

14) Angles and Airwaves - Secret Crowds

I think that we started listening to this right around the Clemson - GT transition years. How about me driving back to Clemson or you driving over to GT every weekend so that we could see each other?

15) Blink 182 - Bored To Death

blink-182 makes a comeback. I burned this one for you too, but then I think we realized you didn't have a CD player in your (first) new Civic (or maybe you did...). We saw them in concert, and they happened to be playing on our wedding anniversary -- April 26, 2017.

16) Taylor Swift - Delicate

I couldn't leave out Taylor - she made the cut. I like this one because of the dark jeans and nikes. I have those things. I also wanted to put New Year's Day here. In fact, I'm going to link to it here as a bonus track. Hold on to the memories.

17) Pinkfong - Baby Shark

So technically these last two aren't in the youtube playlist. It looks like Google won't let you add kid's videos to playlists. I guess they don't want parents to plop kids in front of a huge TV playlist? Silly Google. Here's the link just in case you really need it.

18) Badanamu - Dino Bus

Get on the bus! Ultimate dad song, and here we are, at the end. Just the end of this 18 though. I can't wait for the next. Love you!
